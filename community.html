Gather is built around one simple idea: meaningful friendships begin with shared interests. Whether someone is moving to a new city, searching for a hobby, or simply looking to meet new people, our communities make those first connections easier.

Built Around Real Connections

From photography walks and fitness groups to coffee meetups and gaming nights, every community has one goal—bringing people together.
